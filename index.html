Frequently Asked Questions

LogicJam is a weekend-long hackathon-style event focusing on mathematics and innovation. Each year, we plan to accept several hundred applicants to join us for a weekend in late November to solve several mathematics problems.

Applying to LogicJam is open to all students* aged between 13 and 24 years old interested in mathematics and innovation, regardless of experience level. In the case of in-person opportunities, we do not cover expenses related to travel. In order to participate in our event, you will need to submit an application by July 1, 2024 and wait for acceptance.

*You must be enrolled in an accredited elementary/high school or post-secondary program in November 2024.

Registration opens globally on April 22, 2024 and will remain open until July 1, 2024. Acceptances/rejections are sent out on a rolling basis, with the first decisions coming out in early August 2024. All decisions will be out by September 2024, and the event is in late November 2024.

Both registering and participating (if you get accepted) in LogicJam 2024 will be 100% free. There may be fees related to potential and optional in-person opportunities. Regardless of your income level, as long as you value mathematics and innovation, there's a spot for you here at LogicJam.

LogicJam isn't your typical hackathon; it's a one-of-a-kind experience. It's free to attend, won't interfere with your school schedule, and offers the chance to network, win prizes, and tackle exciting math challenges with team members and hosts in a competitive setting!
