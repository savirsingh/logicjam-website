About LogicJam

Welcome to LogicJam, the premier mathematics event for students! Our event is designed to provide a unique and challenging experience for students interested in mathematics and innovation.

At LogicJam, we believe in the power of mathematics to solve real-world problems and inspire creativity. Our event features a weekend-long hackathon-style format with a focus on problem-solving and collaboration.

Join us this November for an unforgettable experience that combines the thrill of competition with the opportunity to learn from experts in the field of mathematics.
