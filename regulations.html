Challenge Regulations

Here are the regulations for participating in LogicJam:

  • Participants must be between the ages of 13 and 24 years old.
  • Participants must be enrolled in an accredited school or post-secondary program.
  • Registration opens on April 22, 2024, and closes on July 1, 2024.
  • Acceptances/rejections are sent out on a rolling basis starting in early August 2024.
  • All decisions will be finalized by September 2024.
  • The event will take place from November 22-24, 2024.

Please note that all decisions are final and cannot be appealed. If you are not accepted, don't be disheartened - we strive to keep our acceptance rate below 15% to ensure all our resources are given to the top applicants. Apply next year free of charge!
